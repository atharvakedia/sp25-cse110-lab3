New Business

  • Aperture: Controls depth of field. Wider aperture (lower F-stop) allows more light and creates a shallow depth of field.
  • Shutter Speed: Controls motion. Longer shutter speed increases motion blur, shorter shutter speed freezes action.
  • ISO: Controls sensitivity to light. Higher ISO introduces more noise but helps in low-light settings.
